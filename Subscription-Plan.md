# RazeSEED Subscription Plans  
### (Updated Global Pricing Model — Draft v2.0)

## 1. Overview  
RazeSEED offers a globally accessible, AI-powered funding and validation system for founders at all stages.  
To maximize worldwide adoption and affordability, the platform provides **four subscription tiers** priced at  
**$100 / $300 / $600 / $1000**, each offering increasing levels of AI tools, investor visibility, and blockchain-backed trust.

These plans act as the primary entry points for seekers (founders), helping them validate ideas, build pitch assets, generate MVPs, and prepare for crowdfunded or investor-backed funding rounds.
